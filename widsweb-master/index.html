We are here to empower, inspire, and connect women in data science and to create and curate a community for women in technology at UBC.

Our Mission

We will provide you with resources and opportunities for you to improve your data science skills. Throughout this summer and upcoming year, we will be creating workshops , datathons, and symposiums to bring together our community.

Wids @ UBC will host workshops, datathons, projects, and symposiums for members. You will be able to improve your data skills with events on python, r, etc. and learn new topics in order to succeed in the industry as a data professional.
